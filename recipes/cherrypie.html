Cherry Pie

Image credit: Vice

Description

Cherry pie, famously associated with the TV series "Twin Peaks," is a classic American dessert known for its sweet, tart cherry filling encased in a flaky pie crust. In the show, it symbolizes comfort and nostalgia, often enjoyed by characters in the cozy, small-town diner. The iconic cherry pie became a cultural touchstone, representing the blend of wholesome Americana and the show's surreal, mysterious atmosphere. Its rich flavor and vibrant red color make it a beloved treat, both in the series and in real life.

Ingredients

8" Crust:

  • 1-1/2 c. flour
  • 1/2 c. Crisco
  • 1/4 c. ice water

Filling:

  • 3 c. cherries (pitted, sour frozen)
  • 1 c. water
  • 1 c. Baker's sugar
  • 4 T. cornstarch
  • 1/8 t. salt
