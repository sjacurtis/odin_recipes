Cherry Pie

Image credit: Vice

Description

Cherry pie, famously associated with the TV series "Twin Peaks," is a classic American dessert known for its sweet, tart cherry filling encased in a flaky pie crust. In the show, it symbolizes comfort and nostalgia, often enjoyed by characters in the cozy, small-town diner. The iconic cherry pie became a cultural touchstone, representing the blend of wholesome Americana and the show's surreal, mysterious atmosphere. Its rich flavor and vibrant red color make it a beloved treat, both in the series and in real life.

Ingredients

8" Crust:

  • 1-1/2 c. flour
  • 1/2 c. Crisco
  • 1/4 c. ice water

Filling:

  • 3 c. cherries (pitted, sour frozen)
  • 1 c. water
  • 1 c. Baker's sugar
  • 4 T. cornstarch
  • 1/8 t. salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour and Crisco with fork. Add ice water. Mix with your hands.
  2. When blended, roll into ball and refrigerate overnight.
  3. To roll out: flour both rolling pin and flat surface, split ball in two, roll out 1/2 to fit pan and 1/2 for lattice.
  4. Thaw cherries at room temp and strain (yields 2 c. juice). Taste for sweetness, more/less sugar may be needed.
  5. Add 1 c. water to make 3 c. juice (reserve 1 c. juice for cornstarch mix).
  6. Dissolve cornstarch in 1 c. juice, stir with whip.
  7. Combine 2 c. juice, 2/3 c. sugar, salt and bring to a boil.
  8. Add cornstarch mix, cook until clear, about 5 min. (if cooked too long, syrup gets gummy).
  9. Remove from heat; stir in 1/3 c. sugar (blend thoroughly).
  10. Pour mixture over cherries, fold with wooden spoon, cool (stir mix while cooling to prevent scum from forming on top).
  11. Pour mix in pie shell. Top completed pie with lattice crust.
  12. Bake @ 425 F for 35 - 40 min.
