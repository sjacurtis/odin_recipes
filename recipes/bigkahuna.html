Big Kahuna Burger

Description

Ah, the Big Kahuna Burger! This juicy masterpiece feels straight out of a Tarantino flick—bold, dripping with flavor, and utterly unforgettable. Imagine a luscious beef patty nestled in a fluffy sesame seed bun, topped with fresh lettuce and a slice of pineapple that transports your taste buds to a tropical paradise. It's a burger that shouts, “I'm here to make a statement!”

With each bite, you're not just eating; you're experiencing a slice of cinematic glory. It's the kind of burger that makes you feel like you're in a scene from Pulp Fiction, savoring every moment and contemplating the deliciousness of life itself.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or bison
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grill seasoning
  • 4 pineapple rings (canned or fresh)
  • 4 pieces bacon
  • 4 slices monterery jack cheese

Spicy Cilantro Mayo

  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha or chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro
